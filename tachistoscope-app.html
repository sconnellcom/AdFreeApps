What is this tool?

Our free tachistoscope is a memory training tool that briefly displays letters or patterns for you to memorize and recall. This cognitive exercise, also known as rapid serial visual presentation, helps improve visual memory, pattern recognition, and information processing speed - all without the distraction of advertisements.

Whether you're a student looking to improve study skills, an athlete training visual processing, or simply someone interested in cognitive enhancement, this tool provides an effective, scientifically-backed training method.
