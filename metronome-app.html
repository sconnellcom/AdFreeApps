How It Works

Using our metronome is simple and intuitive:

  1. Choose Your Mode: Select between Regular (audio + visual), Silent (visual only), or Auto (intelligent beat detection).
  2. Set Your Tempo: Use the BPM slider to select your desired tempo between 40-240 beats per minute.
  3. Pick a Sound: In Regular mode, choose from six different sound options to match your preference.
  4. Start Practicing: Click the Start button and practice along with the beat.
  5. Track Progress: In Auto mode, watch real-time feedback on your timing accuracy.

Auto Mode - Advanced Practice

The Auto mode is perfect for practicing with acoustic instruments. Place your device on a surface or hold it while you play, and the metronome will detect the physical motion of your playing. It calculates your actual tempo and only beeps when you drift off beat, helping you develop internal timing without becoming dependent on constant audible cues.
