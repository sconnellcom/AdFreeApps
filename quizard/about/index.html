Quizard

The ad-free flashcard study app

What is Quizard?

Quizard is a simple, distraction-free flashcard app inspired by the classic Quizlet flashcard experience. Create decks, add cards, and study using a tried-and-true flip-and-rate loop — completely free, with no ads and no account required.

Features

  • 🃏 Create unlimited decks with any number of text cards
  • 👆 Tap to flip each card and reveal the answer
  • ✅ Mark cards as "Know" or "Still Learning" after each flip
  • 🔄 "Still Learning" cards reappear later in the same session
  • 🔀 Optional shuffle mode to randomize card order
  • 📊 Session summary with mastery percentage and review count
  • 💾 All data saved locally in your browser — no sign-in needed
  • ⌨️ Keyboard shortcuts: Space to flip, 1 = Know, 2 = Still Learning

How to Use

1. Tap New Deck on the home screen and give it a title.
2. Add cards with a term on the front and a definition on the back.
3. Tap Study on any deck to start a session.
4. Tap the card to flip it, then choose Know or Still Learning.
5. Missed cards come back later. Keep going until your queue is empty!

Privacy

All your decks and cards are stored only in your browser's local storage. Nothing is sent to a server. No accounts. No tracking.
