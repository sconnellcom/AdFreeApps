A strategic tile-placement game with 3D tiles

About

Castles is a strategic tile-placement game inspired by Carcassonne. Build a medieval landscape by placing tiles with roads, cities, grass fields, and castles. Each tile features 3D visual effects to bring your world to life.

How to Play

Basic Rules:

  • Place Tiles: Click on an empty grid cell to place your current tile
  • Match Edges: Tile edges must match the adjacent tiles (road to road, city to city, etc.)
  • Rotate Tiles: Click the "Rotate Tile" button to rotate your current tile before placing it
  • Strategic Placement: Think ahead about how tiles will connect to create larger features

Features

  • 3D Tile Graphics: Each tile features depth and shading for a 3D appearance
  • 72 Unique Tiles: Various combinations of roads, cities, grass, and castles
  • 11x11 Grid: Plenty of space to build your medieval landscape
  • Tile Rotation: Rotate tiles to fit them perfectly into your landscape
  • Edge Matching: Automatic validation ensures tiles match their neighbors
  • Game Stats: Track your progress with tiles placed and remaining
  • Theme Support: Choose from 12 different color themes
  • No Ads or Tracking: Enjoy a clean, distraction-free experience

Tile Types

The game includes four types of terrain features:

  • Grass (Green): Open fields and meadows
  • Road (Brown): Paths connecting different parts of the landscape
  • City (Gray): Walled settlements and fortifications
  • Castle (Purple): Special fortress structures

Tips for Success

  • Plan ahead - think about which tiles might come next
  • Try to keep your options open by avoiding dead ends
  • Rotate tiles to find the best placement option
  • Start from the center and expand outward strategically
  • Match edges carefully to avoid getting stuck

Privacy & Technical Details

Castles is a client-side application that runs entirely in your browser. No data is collected, tracked, or sent to any server. Your game progress is stored locally in your browser.

Technologies used: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Canvas API
