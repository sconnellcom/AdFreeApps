About Ad Free Apps

Why AdFreeApps.com?

AdFreeApps.com was created to provide simple, useful web apps for myself, my kids, and my friends. I wanted tools that were easy to use without having to install anything, and most importantly, without being bombarded by advertisements or having my data tracked.

These are straightforward tools that solve everyday needs - from practicing music with a metronome, to drawing, to keeping track of game scores. No complexity, no distractions, just what you need.

Your Privacy Matters

All settings are saved in your browser's local storage. This means everything stays on your device - we don't have access to your data, and we won't try to access it. Your preferences, settings, and any data you create within these apps lives entirely on your device.

What this means for you:

  • Your data is completely private
  • No tracking, no analytics, no cookies
  • Works offline once loaded
  • If you clear your browser data or use a different device, your settings will be lost
