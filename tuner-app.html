How It Works

Tuning your instrument is simple and straightforward:

  1. Grant Microphone Access: When prompted, allow the app to access your device's microphone.
  2. Position Your Instrument: Hold your instrument near the microphone or place your device close to the sound hole/body.
  3. Play a String: Pluck or bow a single string and let it ring clearly.
  4. Read the Display: The tuner shows the detected note and whether it's sharp (too high) or flat (too low).
  5. Adjust and Repeat: Turn the tuning peg to adjust the pitch until the indicator shows you're perfectly in tune.
